WORKSHOP LIMITS

This is the first year we try organizing a workshop day the day before the conference and we have clearly failed in our communication about this.

Once you have bought your conference ticket you will be asked to register a place for the workshop. That's because the number of places for the workshops is limited:

  • workshops take more space than conferences, you need seats, tables and electrical outlets
  • one workshop facilitator can only help a limited amount number of people
  • workshops take more time to prepare and we received less proposals than for conferences

We are fully aware of the inconvenience this can cause,for people who have already bought their tickets and organized their trip but may not be able to get in a workshop. We humbly offer our apologies for it and are trying to make up for it by finding an alternate activity for people in this situation.
